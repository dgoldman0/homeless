# Literature source access notes

These notes record accessible source records used by the paper. Paywalled full text is not bundled.

## Primary literature records

- Morris and Thorne (1988), *American Journal of Physics*, DOI `10.1119/1.15620`. DOI record used for bibliographic metadata.
- Morris, Thorne, and Yurtsever (1988), *Physical Review Letters*, DOI `10.1103/PhysRevLett.61.1446`.
- Ford and Roman (1996), *Physical Review D*, DOI `10.1103/PhysRevD.53.5496`. APS and OSTI records provide abstract, citation metadata, and the key quantum-inequality conclusion used in the paper.
- Fewster and Roman (2005), *Physical Review D*, DOI `10.1103/PhysRevD.72.044023`; arXiv `gr-qc/0507013`.
- Hochberg and Visser (1998), *Physical Review Letters*, DOI `10.1103/PhysRevLett.81.746`; arXiv `gr-qc/9802048`.
- Alcubierre (1994), *Classical and Quantum Gravity*, DOI `10.1088/0264-9381/11/5/001`.
- Pfenning and Ford (1997), *Classical and Quantum Gravity*, DOI `10.1088/0264-9381/14/7/011`; arXiv `gr-qc/9702026`.
- Everett and Roman (1997), *Physical Review D*, DOI `10.1103/PhysRevD.56.2100`. APS abstract records the causal-control issue, Krasnikov infrastructure interpretation, and negative-energy-layer conclusion.
- Van Den Broeck (1999), *Classical and Quantum Gravity*, DOI `10.1088/0264-9381/16/12/314`; arXiv `gr-qc/9905084`.

- Fuchs et al. (2024), *Classical and Quantum Gravity*, DOI `10.1088/1361-6382/ad26aa`. Monash publication metadata provides abstract and DOI.
- Gao, Jafferis, and Wall (2017), *Journal of High Energy Physics*, DOI `10.1007/JHEP12(2017)151`; Springer record is open access and records the double-trace deformation mechanism and negative averaged null energy.
