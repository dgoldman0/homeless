Relation to this paper: motivates infrastructure-based transit and highlights the control problem for onboard management of a warp bubble wall.

Verified abstract excerpt (metadata page): the observer at the center of Alcubierre's bubble is causally separated from the bubble wall; Krasnikov-tube infrastructure avoids that first difficulty but still involves negative energy and causality concerns.
